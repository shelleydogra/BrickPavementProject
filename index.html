overview


This projects goal was to use BARR antique brick to lay a pathway next to the drive way. Also to direct the water away from the house. Lay down the electrical line for a lamp post and then install the lamp post itself.

The project took about a month to complete with 2-3 hours of labor, on average, a week.

I did the project with my neighbor on his property in the summer of 2013. It was a hard project, very intensive hard labor, but we saved a lot of money. The most important thing was that I made a bunch of cold hard CASH!
